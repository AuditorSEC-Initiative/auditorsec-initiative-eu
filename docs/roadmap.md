# AuditorSEC Initiative EU — 36-Month Programme Roadmap

> **Programme:** Europe’s Web3 AI Security Core | **Budget Target:** €25M (Horizon-style) | **Base:** Ukraine / Diia.City

---

## Vision

Position Ukraine as a leading European R&D hub for Web3 security, AI governance, post-quantum cryptography, and digital trust infrastructure — delivering open standards, enterprise tooling, and trained talent for EU digital markets.
